About Recursive Sequence

Recursive Sequence is a free educational math tool website focused on number sequences. Its calculators help students, teachers, and independent learners check arithmetic and geometric sequence calculations without hiding the formulas behind the answers.

Our purpose

The site is designed to make common sequence tasks easier to explore: generating terms, finding an nth term, and calculating a finite sum. Each calculator is paired with explanations, examples, and reminders about common mistakes so it can serve as a learning aid as well as a quick calculation tool.

How the content is prepared

The educational material is written specifically for this website in clear English. We aim to present standard mathematical ideas accurately and in a practical form. Calculator results should still be reviewed in the context of your assignment, lesson, or real-world problem.

Start calculating

Use the arithmetic sequence calculator for a constant difference, or the geometric sequence calculator for a constant ratio.
